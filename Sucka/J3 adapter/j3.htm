Logic analyzer capture of J3 service port signals

Snapshots of MBUS activity on the J3 service port of an EEC-IV and EEC-V were captured with a logic analyzer. Here is a screen shot of the EEC-IV capture:


 

For a better look at what's happening, download a complete capture and this viewer.

Here is the EEC-IV complete capture (32768 samples at 50ns intervals). It was taken from an SD20 EEC-IV.

Here is the EEC-V complete capture (32768 samples at 25ns intervals). It was taken from a VFA2 EEC-V off a '98 Mustang GT 4.6L-2V Auto

Unzip the capture and viewer into the same directory, run the PCLADEMO viewer software, press "L" on the main screen and enter "SD20" or "VFA2" as the file name to load (without the quotes). Press F1 to see the traces.While viewing the traces, press "H" to see a help menu for a list of keys used to move around the screen.

For more info on the logic analyser, visit the designer's web-page.
